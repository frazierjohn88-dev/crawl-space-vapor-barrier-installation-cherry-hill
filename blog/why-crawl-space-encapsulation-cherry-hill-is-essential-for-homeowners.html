Understanding Crawl Space Encapsulation in Cherry Hill

Crawl space encapsulation involves completely sealing your crawl space from the outside environment using high-quality vapor barriers, proper insulation, and moisture control systems. This comprehensive approach addresses multiple issues that commonly plague Cherry Hill homes.

Benefits of Professional Crawl Space Encapsulation

  • Reduced energy costs by up to 15-20%
  • Improved indoor air quality
  • Prevention of mold and mildew growth
  • Enhanced structural integrity
  • Increased home value

Why Cherry Hill Homes Need Crawl Space Encapsulation

New Jersey's humid climate makes our area particularly susceptible to crawl space moisture issues. Our professional inspection services regularly reveal that unprotected crawl spaces can lead to serious problems.

Common Issues Prevented by Encapsulation

  • Moisture accumulation and wood rot
  • Pest infestations
  • High energy bills
  • Poor indoor air quality
  • Foundation problems

The Encapsulation Process

Our professional encapsulation service includes:

  1. Thorough inspection and assessment
  2. Removal of existing debris and damaged materials
  3. Installation of proper drainage systems
  4. Application of high-quality vapor barriers
  5. Installation of dehumidification systems when needed

We often combine encapsulation with crawl space waterproofing for maximum protection.
